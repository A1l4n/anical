# Privacy Policy — AniCal

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

AniCal ("the app") is a fan-made anime airing calendar. This policy explains
what data is collected, used, and stored.

**Short version: AniCal collects nothing about you. All your data stays on
your device.**

---

## Data We Collect

### Data we do NOT collect
- No account or login is required
- No personal information (name, email, phone) is ever requested
- No usage analytics or crash reporting is sent to our servers
- No advertising identifiers are collected
- No location data is accessed

### Data stored locally on your device only
The following data is saved in your device's local storage and never leaves
your device:

| Data | Purpose | Stored where |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Favorited anime IDs | Show your watchlist | Device localStorage |
| Timezone preference | Convert JST to your local time | Device localStorage |
| Notification settings | Alert timing preferences | Device localStorage |
| Schedule cache | Offline access for up to 4 hours | Device localStorage |

You can delete all of this at any time by clearing the app's data in your
device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

---

## Third-Party Services

### Jikan API (jikan.moe)
AniCal fetches anime schedule data from the Jikan API, a free public API
that provides MyAnimeList data.

- Requests are made directly from your device to api.jikan.moe
- Your IP address is sent to Jikan's servers as part of the network request
  (this is how the internet works — no app can avoid this)
- Jikan's own privacy policy applies: https://jikan.moe

### MyAnimeList
Schedule data originates from MyAnimeList (myanimelist.net). AniCal is not
affiliated with MyAnimeList. Their privacy policy applies to their service.

---

## Children's Privacy

AniCal does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, including
children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no data submission
forms, and no way to contact us through the app.
