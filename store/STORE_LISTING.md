## Full Description

Never miss an episode again.

AniCal is a clean, fast anime airing calendar that shows the full seasonal
schedule — no account, no login, no tracking. Just open it and see what's on.

──────────────────────────────────────
📅 WEEKLY SCHEDULE
──────────────────────────────────────
• Full seasonal lineup organized by day of the week
• Powered by MyAnimeList data via the free Jikan API
• Automatic JST → your local timezone conversion for every show
• Shows sorted by airtime so you always know what's next
• Search across the full schedule instantly

──────────────────────────────────────
⭐ FAVORITES & LIVE COUNTDOWN
──────────────────────────────────────
• Save shows to your watchlist with one tap (☆ → ★)
• Live countdown timers: "in 2h 15m", "in 45m", "AIRING NOW"
• Green LIVE pulse indicator for shows broadcasting right now
• Countdowns refresh every 30 seconds, always accurate

──────────────────────────────────────
🔔 SMART NOTIFICATIONS
──────────────────────────────────────
• Get notified before your favorites air — choose 0, 5, 10, 15, 30 or 60 min
• Per-show notification toggle — turn off alerts for specific shows
• Works even when the app is in the background
• Test notification button so you can verify it works

──────────────────────────────────────
📋 MY LIST TAB
──────────────────────────────────────
• Your watchlist bucketed automatically: 🔴 Live → ⏰ Next 12h → 🌅 Today → 📅 This week
• At-a-glance stats: total shows this season, airing today, top genre
• One-tap access to Crunchyroll for any favorited show

──────────────────────────────────────
🗓️ MONTH CALENDAR VIEW
──────────────────────────────────────
• Full month-at-a-glance calendar
• Color dots show which days have anime — orange for your favorites
• Tap any day to see that day's full lineup

──────────────────────────────────────
🌐 TIMEZONE SUPPORT
──────────────────────────────────────
• Auto-detects your device timezone
• 19 manual presets: US (East/Central/West), UK, Europe, Japan, Korea,
  India, Southeast Asia, Australia, Brazil, Mexico, Dubai

──────────────────────────────────────
✨ EVERYTHING ELSE
──────────────────────────────────────
• Offline support — 4-hour schedule cache so it works without internet
• Dark UI optimized for night watching sessions
• No ads, no account, no subscription, no tracking
• Your favorites and settings are saved locally on your device

──────────────────────────────────────

Data provided by MyAnimeList via the Jikan API (jikan.moe).
AniCal is a fan-made app, not affiliated with or endorsed by MyAnimeList.
